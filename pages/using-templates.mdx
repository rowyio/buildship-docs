## Exploring Templates

Click on the "**Templates**" button located in the bottom left corner of the BuildShip App.

Upon entering the Templates section, you'll find a diverse range of categorized templates. These categories include AI,
utilities, internal tools, database, bot, and payment-related templates, making it convenient to find a suitable
template for your workflow.

<Image src={template1} alt='template1' width={1200} />

## Using Templates

- Browse through the available templates and choose the one that aligns with your workflow needs.

- Each template provides a brief overview and associated notes, offering additional guidance on customizing and
  utilizing the template effectively.

- After selecting a template, clone it to create a personalized copy that you can modify and expand upon.

<Callout type='warning'>
  For some templates, you may be prompted to add required fields such as API Keys or Prompts after the cloning process.
</Callout>

<Image src={template2} alt='template2' width={1200} />

## Shipping the templates

Once your BuildShip Template is configured to your specifications, you can proceed to ship your workflow. Click on the
"**Ship**" button located in the top right corner of the BuildShip App.

Shipping the workflow generates an API endpoint that can easily integrate into your applications or products.
