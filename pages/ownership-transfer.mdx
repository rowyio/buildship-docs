# Transferring Workspace Ownership

Transferring ownership of a workspace allows you to give another member full control over the workspace, including
administrative privileges. Follow these steps to transfer ownership:

<Steps>
  ### Navigate to Workspace Settings
    Click on the menu icon (â˜°) in the top left corner and select <b>Workspace Settings</b> from the sidebar.
    <Image src={settings} alt="Workspace Settings navigation" width={800} height={400} />

### Go to the Danger Zone

    In the settings sidebar, ensure you are on the <b>General</b> tab. Scroll down to the <b>Danger zone</b> section.

### Select a New Owner

    Under <b>Transfer ownership</b>, click the dropdown labeled "Select the new owner". A list of current workspace members will appear. Select the member you want to transfer ownership to.
    <Image src={selectOwner} alt="Select new owner" width={800} height={400} />

### Confirm the Transfer

    After selecting the new owner, click the <b>Transfer Ownership</b> button. You may be asked to confirm your choice. Once confirmed, the new owner will have full control over the workspace. You will remain a member, but will lose owner privileges.

</Steps>
